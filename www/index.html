How will your middle school be affected by de Blasio’s SHSAT plan?

This is a database of SHSAT offers to NYC 8th graders for offers of admission for the Fall 2018 school year. You can sort the table by any of its fields.

Of particular note: The 7% cap column shows how many students from that school would be admitted to specialized high schools under Mayor de Blasio’s plan. The column following the 7% cap, the Gain/Loss column, is a calculation that shows how many more or less students the school would send to specialized high school.
